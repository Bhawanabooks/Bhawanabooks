Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your store hours?

We're open daily from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Sunday to Friday. On Saturdays, we open from 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM. We're always here to serve you!

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk orders. Schools, offices, and institutions get competitive rates. Contact us for a custom quote.

What if the product is out of stock?

We can order most items for you. Contact us with your requirements, and we'll arrange delivery within 2-3 business days.

How can I request a special order?

You can visit us in person, call us at 01-4531802 or 9851186573, or use the "Get a Quote" feature on our website. Simply provide the title of the book or the specific stationery brand you need.

Can I deliver orders outside Kathmandu?

Yes, we can arrange shipping across Nepal through courier partners. Please contact us for delivery charges and timelines for your location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, as well as popular digital wallets like eSewa and QR payment for your convenience.
